# Call testing

stage size 800 600

setup
    log starting...

begin
    log calling tester
    call tester one two three
    log tester called

on call tester
    log I am tester $ARGS
    call inner hi there
    pause 1
    log 1
    pause 1
    log 2
    pause 1
    log 3

on call inner
    log inner called with $ARGS
    pause 1
    log inner 1
