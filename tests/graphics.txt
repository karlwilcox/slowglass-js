# Graphic elements test

setup
    log expect grid overall
    graphic create grid as grid
    place grid at centre

    graphic create rect as rectangle 100 200
    place rect at 150 150

    graphic fill white
    graphic create circ as circle 20
    place circ at 400 150

    graphic stroke width 5
    graphic create poly5 as polygon 5 50
    place poly5 at 600 150

    graphic stroke red

    place star1 at 150 400
    log red ellipse at bottom middle

    place elli at 400 450
